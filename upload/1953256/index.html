SHAKE SHAKE

动摇

Designer 吴建兴

这款凳子通过IKEA的木制杂志架、置物台以及花盆拼合而成，其中杂志架作为凳面通过IKEA弹力绳与作为支持的置物台进行连接，木制花盆垫高使其达到适宜使用的高度。

使用者坐上这把凳子的时候，由于弹力绳的弹力会使得凳子坐面摇晃，产生陷入和弹起的感觉。通过不断改变自己的坐姿，坐面陷入的程度也会不同，使得使用者可以改变力度来体验不同程度的回弹感。

This stool is made up of IKEA's wooden magazine racks, mounts, and flowerpots, wherein the magazine racks are connected as stools by IKEA elastic ropes and as supporting mounts, and the wooden pot mats are high effaced to make them suitable for use.

When the user sits on the stool, the elasticity of the elastic rope will make the stool sit on the rocking surface, resulting in the feeling of falling and bouncing. By constantly changing their sitting posture, the degree of sitting will be different, allowing users to change the intensity to experience different degrees of rebound.
