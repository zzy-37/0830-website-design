CARROUSEL 木马

谭禹辰 1951933

不同的坐具高度能提供不同的体验感受。而鞋拔子的高度使体验者跨坐在板凳上，颇具旋转木马的意味，故得名“木马”。

Details 板凳细部

利用鞋拔子做凳腿支撑，螺丝固定在中轴上， 凳腿倒置形成扶手，结合鞋拔子本身的高度提供较高的坐姿。每条凳腿通过底部圆环用螺丝连接，前后均加叠垫片提供更大的接触面积，同时圆环为使用者提供踩踏处。凳面的弧形与鞋拔子相匹配，呼应整体设计语言。

Using shoe pullers to do stool leg support, screws fixed on central shafts, combined with the height of the shoe puller itself to provide higher sitting position. Each stool leg is screwed through the bottom ring, which is stacked with gaskets at the front and rear to provide a larger contact area, while the ring provides the user with a stepping place. The arc of the stool faces matches the shoe puller, echoing the overall design language.

Concept 设计理念

此次设计除凳面为额外加工，其余部件均为宜家购买的现成材料。所有部件均可自行组装，为使用者提供了自己组装的可能，颇具黑客意味。通过鞋拔子的支撑，为使用者提供了不同于矮板凳的坐姿体验，也希望能打破普通板凳给人们带来的刻板印象。

The design is additionally processed, with all parts made of ready-made materials purchased by IKEA. All parts can be assembled on their own, giving users the possibility to assemble them themselves, which is hacked. Through the support of shoe pull, for the user to provide a different from the low bench sitting experience, but also hope to break the ordinary bench to bring people stereotypes.
