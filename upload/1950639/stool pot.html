发现秩序之美，打破、重构秩序是我认为的黑客精神。这次的坐具设计中，我发现了宜家浇水壶的秩序之美，有生命力和流动性的线条赋予了它秩序的自然美，使其在能浇花的同时也能成为装饰品。我延续了它的秩序，通过解构、阵列、嵌套、堆叠的方式，创造出坐面和支撑结构，内化为属于壶凳的秩序之美。

Discovering the beauty of order, breaking and reconstructing order is the hacker spirit in my view. In the design of this stool, I found the beautiful order of Ikea's watering pot. The lines of vitality and fluidity endowed it with the order of nature, which made it also can become an ornament. I continue its order by deconstructing, arraying, nesting and stacking, creating a sitting face and supporting structure, internalizing into my stool.
