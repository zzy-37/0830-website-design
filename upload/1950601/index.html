个人主页

黑客精神

Hacker Spirit

我认为黑客精神是对传统事物的解构与重构，以自由与开放的态度进行创造，从而达到令人意想不到的效果。因此，我决定从本质属性上“黑客”原材料，使之有着和看起来截然不同的使用体验。

设计说明

Design Concept

在这把“曲枕凳”的设计中，我使用了六个软性材质的IKEA颈枕，借由内部置入的的刚性结构完成支撑人体的凳子基础功能，并试图重新定义软性材质在结构连接中的作用：利用其挤压产生的弹力完成三个独立模块间的连接：两个单体颈枕垂直穿插组合成为一个模块，再由三个模块组合成为这把凳子。

I believe that Hacker Spirit should be the deconstruction and reconstitution of conventional products or events with a free and open mind, so as to achieve unexpected outcomes. Therefore, I decided to hack the essence of raw materials, turning which into something entirely different as it seems.

In this project , I took use of 6 IKEA neck pillows and inserted steel structure to support human’s body. I tried to redefine the role of soft texture in structure connection by taking advantage of its elasticity to connect three independent modules. Two single pillows were joined as a module, three modules were combined to be the CHANNEL stool.
