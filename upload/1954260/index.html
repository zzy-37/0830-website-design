ROLLING STOOL

辗转凳

Designer 方馨悦

我的个人主页：https://zzy-37.github.io

ROLLING STOOL打破了“久坐会使肌肉得不到锻炼”的固有认知。凳身由宜家的MAGASIN擀面杖与GRUBBAN踏脚凳的支撑结构组成，保留了擀面杖滚动按压的功能，使之与使用者的臀部和腿部肌肉进行交互，实现锻炼按摩的功效。

ROLLING STOOL breaks down the stereotype that sitting for too long deprives your muscles of exercise. The stool body is composed of the IKEA Magasin rolling pin and the Grubban footstool supporting structure, which retains the rolling and pressing function of the rolling pin, allowing it to interact with the user's buttocks and leg muscles to achieve the effect of exercise and massage.
