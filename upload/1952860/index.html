SKOGSVIKENA

折中凳

Designer 袁佳琳

Personal website: https://yuan-jl.github.io/

折中，中西文化的碰撞与交融
折中，用折线汇集成中式纹样
折中，不偏不倚、俏皮的庄重

运用黑客思维，挑战文化的不可能，以北欧风格的宜家门钩搭建具有中式风格的板凳——折中凳。哑光黑色的钢制门钩通过钻孔、螺丝固定形成中式几何纹饰，四面围合成闭合空间，作为板凳的支撑结构；以松木作为椅面。板凳呈正方体块状，整体呈现庄重感。

I utilize the thinking mode of hacker to challenge the impossibility of culture and build a Chinese-style bench with Nordic-style IKEA hooks(SKOGVIKEN)--SKOGSVIKENA. The steel door hook with matte black is drilled and screwed to form Chinese geometric decoration. The enclosed space is used as a supporting structure for the bench. Pine wood is used as the surface of the chair. The bench is a cube shape, presenting a solemn feeling.
