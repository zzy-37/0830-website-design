这是一个可以移动，可以“唱歌”的凳子，选取的材料是宜家的盘子滤干架，塑料袋，挂钩。利用盘架的空隙，捆绑塑料袋以制成椅面，通过宜家的不锈钢制，铁制挂钩进行碰撞发声。使用者坐在上面进行移动，凳子便会发出美妙的声音。在承重方面，为达到椅面坐上滑动时保持平衡，凳子后方的金属挂件整体重量大于前侧。 形态方面模拟钢琴键盘的形态，利用宜家废弃的保鲜袋卷成筒状，达到减震和造型感的作用。

This is a movable stool that can "sing". The materials selected are IKEA dish drain racks, plastic bags, and hooks. Utilizing the gaps in the tray frame, bind plastic bags to make the chair surface, and use IKEA's stainless steel and iron hooks to make a collision sound. When the user sits on it and moves, the stool will make a beautiful sound. In terms of load-bearing, in order to maintain balance when the seat surface slides on the seat, the overall weight of the metal pendant at the back of the stool is greater than the front side. In terms of form, it simulates the form of a piano keyboard, using IKEA's discarded fresh-keeping bag to roll it into a cylindrical shape to achieve the effect of shock absorption and styling.
