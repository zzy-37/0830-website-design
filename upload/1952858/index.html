这款凳子基于IKEA波昂（POÄNG）系列中的脚凳改造而成，将原本靠近座面的连接杆移到了接近底部的位置，并仿照连接杆的固定结构，设计了3D打印的零件，在两条蹬腿之间额外增加了4根金属杆，最后将IKEA的瓦瑞拉（VARIERA）抽屉垫缠绕于其上，形成凳面。

宽大的椅面使其坐上去非常舒适，EVA塑料材质的抽屉垫有类似皮革的触感且富有弹性，为用户提供了惬意且有趣的体验。凳面采用“之”字型的结构，加上EVA塑料与金属杆之间的摩擦力，使得凳面富有弹性，可以贴合臀部曲线的同时，也十分牢固和可靠。最底部的连接杆可以上下移动，来调节凳面的总体张力，适应不同的需求。

This stool is based on the Footstool from IKEA’s POÄNG series. The connecting rod that was originally close to the seat surface is moved to a position close to the bottom. A 3D printed part is designed imitating the original fixing structure so that 4 additional metal rods are added between the two stool legs. Finally, IKEA’s VARIERA drawer mat is wrapped around the rods to form a surface.

The extra-large stool surface makes it very comfortable to sit on. The drawer pad made of EVA plastic material has a leather-like touch with high flexibility, providing a satisfying and interesting sitting experience. The surface adopts a Z-like structure, coupled with the friction between plastic and metal, makes the surface elastic enough to fit the curves of the hip, while being stable and reliable at the same time. The connecting rod at the bottom can be moved up and down to adjust the overall tension of the stool surface to meet different needs.
