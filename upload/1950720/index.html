Roll up and sit!
It's Rolla Stool

地毯包裹木质结构支撑出坐面，从右向左卷起得到板凳。让人在需要板凳时能够方便坐下，不需要时放平作为地毯使用不占用空间。使用宜家地毯“朗斯鲁普”和木材制作而成，色彩明亮，形式单纯。践行“用可能的手法达到看似不可能目的”的黑客精神。
Rolling it up from right to left, people can sit easily when they need a stool, and it can be laid flat and used as a carpet without taking up space. The seating surface is supported by a wooden structure wrapped under a carpet. Made from IKEA carpet " RANGSTRUP" and wood, it is bright in color and simple in style. Practice the hacker spirit of "using possible means to achieve seemingly impossible goals".
