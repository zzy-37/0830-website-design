「A」STOOL

板·凳

Designer 张竞文

www.personal-website.com


黑客精神是忠实于一件物体，通过对其构建有效的新秩序，将力量有限的单薄个体组合为强大的整体。「A」的设计利用竹板轻便及坚硬的层压纤维结构，通过重复插接和曲面的设计语言，12块层压竹板经过精密计算和手工切割，在无钉无胶下实现高强度连接，充满放射性几何美感的同时节约其运输成本。

原承重手机的支架希格芬已能够稳定支撑人的重量，并兼具功能与造型。亚克力凳面完美卡入凳身，诚实地展现内部结构。

完全由个体相互支撑而成立的「A」可以成为你最安全的支点。



Hack spirit means to be loyal to an object. By constructing an effective order for it, it combines the thin individuals with limited strength into a powerful whole. In design of「a」, I made use of the lightweight and rigid laminated fibre structure of the bamboo board. By repeating the design language of the splice and curved surface, the original load-bearing mobile phone bracket Sigfinn has been able to support the weight of a person stably, and has both function and form. The acrylic board is perfectly wedged into the body of the stool, revealing the internal structure in an honest way. The 「A」 , which is formed entirely by individuals supporting each other, can be your safest fulcrum to sit.
