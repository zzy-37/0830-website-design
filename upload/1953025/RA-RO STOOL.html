RA-RO STOOL

Designer 辛其蔚

Zn-7vv.github.io

椅子由象征束缚的藤条以及象征混乱的麻绳编制而成，
层层叠叠的编织效果呈现出重重阻挠但又目标清晰的视觉冲击，
反映勇于突破限制、探索未知，
同时在复杂世界的混沌中保持清晰自我的逻辑的黑客精神；

实现坐具功能的同时，增加双层储物及桌几功能，
多功能的使用特性使人们的生活更加便捷美好。

The chair is made of rattan symbolizing bondage and hemp rope symbolizing chaos.
The layer upon layer braiding presents a sense of obstruction but clear goal.
It reflects the hacker spirit that to have the courage to break through restrictions
and to explore the unknown, while maintaining clear logic in a complex world.

While realizing the function of the seat, the double-layer storage and table functions are added as well.
Its multi-functional features make people's life more convenient and beautiful.
